Define the goals of the recovery plan

Define recovery objectives and manage all assets and facilities in an integrated manner. Select the corresponding recovery method. Prioritize the protection of critical operators to prevent "gaps" in critical operations.

Posted on 11-29-2022
Create the recovery system

Creating a recovery system should be designed for the specific target type and backup method. And use multi-factor authorization or multi-step identity verification to prevent hacking. When you have the ability, choose multiple locations for redundant backups to each other.

Posted on 11-29-2022
Perform relevant tests on the entire recovery plan

Conducting the appropriate walkthroughs before officially putting this recovery plan into use will help identify its current vulnerabilities and flaws and fix them. Even after the recovery plan is officially operational, it will need to be maintained at a fixed frequency. This is to prevent all kinds of failures due to slack personnel and aging hardware.
