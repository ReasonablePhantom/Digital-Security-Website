Perform relevant tests on the entire recovery plan

Date: 11-29-2022

Conducting the appropriate walkthroughs before officially putting this recovery plan into use will help identify its current vulnerabilities and flaws and fix them. Even after the recovery plan is officially operational, it will need to be maintained at a fixed frequency. This is to prevent all kinds of failures due to slack personnel and aging hardware.
