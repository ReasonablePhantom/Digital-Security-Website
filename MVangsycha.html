Digital Security:

Firewall Protection

The First Line of Defense in Digital Security

Introduction:

Explanation of firewall implementation and usage in daily environments such as home or business computers. Brief explanation of how a firewall works that leads into more detail in Contents.

Data Transmission:

Data and information is always being transferred from one place to another. The process of moving it from one place to another takes a lot of time and energy but with technology it only takes seconds. However, this leads to unprotected transfer of information. -lead into firewall security…

Firewall Types:

There are many types of firewalls. Some restrict the flow of information to check every detail found. Some let everything come through without checking. It all depends on what kind of hardware and security is needed for the job. -Explain types of firewalls and then lead to the pros/cons below

Data Blockage & Threats:

Firewalls can restrict a lot of information. This is due to the increasing number of threats that can be passed through older firewalls. There is always another way into a computer system and because of that firewalls can also be very strict. -Brief explanation of viruses/malware and how firewalls stop them or get through.

Conclusion:

Explanation of how a firewall is always filtering out bad data and information however it does not get all of it due to the increasing amounts of new ways to get past firewalls. This is where other types of systems come into play, etc.
